🌀 NewTime

Cycle.00.00.00.00.000

NewTime is an alternative time system starting at the dawn of our era: 0000-01-01T00:00:00Z.

It redefines time as a modular structure, no longer bound to Earth’s orbit or solar days:

  • Cycle — synthetic "year", with 12 Phases
  • Phase — like months, 30 Frames per Phase
  • Frame — 6 Ticks per Frame
  • Tick — synthetic "day", 720 SubTicks
  • SubTick — ≈1.2 seconds (replacing the "second")

No leap years. No daylight saving. No time zones. Only absolute, globally-synced logic.

💠 How to Read NewTime

Cycle.34228.01.12.03.456 — time since Year 0:

  • Cycle — years since epoch (0000)
  • Phase — month-alike block (12 per Cycle)
  • Frame — subdivision (30 per Phase)
  • Tick — synthetic "days" (6 per Frame)
  • SubTick — ≈1.2s logical pulse (720 per Tick)

NewTime offers structure without sky. A purely algorithmic calendar.

🧭 Working Windows: Time Without Zones

In NewTime, there's no such thing as "local time".

Everyone ticks to the same global rhythm — but activity windows remain.

These are Working Windows: agreed-upon ranges of Tick numbers within a Frame.

Example:

  • Work: Tick 01–04 — 80% of a “day”, used for active hours
  • Rest: Tick 05–00 — recharge, leisure, sleep

Cities, teams, or even individuals define their own windows.
You don’t change the time — you change your habits.

Tokyo and Berlin no longer argue about "time zones".
They just start working at different Ticks.
